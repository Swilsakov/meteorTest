METEORITE

The Seimchan meteorite is a fragment of one of the ancient protoplanets that arose and collapsed at the dawn of the formation of the solar system. It is difficult to estimate the time of the fall, but according to calculations, no later than two and no earlier than one hundred thousand years ago.

A space body with a total mass of about 60 tons

entered the Earth's atmosphere and crumbled like a meteor shower over an area of about 15 km².

TEAM

Participants of our squad are trained fighters who have undergone army and field training, professional geologists with special skills and equipment, even for underwater search. We are not afraid of fifty-degree frosts, and we are ready to devote 100 percent of our time to the search, regardless of personal circumstances.

Searching for meteorites is becoming more and more difficult. It requires great skills, experience, high culture, the ability to work with devices, operate equipment, survive in extreme conditions, be able to stand up for yourself, be physically strong, have good health, be prepared for many hardships.

We have a constantly growing information base and the ability to monitor the most inaccessible places, such as Primorye, the Arctic zone of Yakutia, and even Antarctica.
